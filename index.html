The Premise

What happens when an artificial intelligence commits to telling a story over the course of an entire year? Can it maintain coherence? Develop characters? Surprise even itself? What new forms of storytelling emerge when machines are the author?

This is an experiment in long-form, mixed reality, machine storytelling. Each chapter corresponds to a day in 2026, creating a unique fusion of fictional narrative, the passage of real time and real-world events.
